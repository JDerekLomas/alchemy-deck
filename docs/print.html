Alchemy Deck - Printable Oracle Cards

34 oracle cards + 5 prompt cards | Print double-sided, flip on long edge

Quotes from primary sources at sourcelibrary.org

Stage
Nigredo
Blackening
Ravens circle a black sun. The old form dies in the vessel. What must you face in the darkness? What shadow holds the seed of change?
"I am black, white, yellow, and red." —Maier
Stage
Albedo
Whitening
A white dove emerges from darkness. The blackened matter is washed to silver-white. What clarity is emerging? What is being purified?
"Water purifies all imperfect things." —Sendivogius
Stage
Citrinitas
Yellowing
Golden light breaks over the horizon. The white matter yellows as consciousness stirs. What wisdom is dawning? What first light do you glimpse?
"The vulture cries: I am white and black, yellow and red." —Maier
Stage
Rubedo
Reddening
Phoenix rises from flames. Sun and moon unite in sacred marriage. The red stone is born. What wholeness is possible? What is ready to be complete?
"The fixed becomes volatile; the volatile, fixed." —Khunrath
Operation
Calcination
Burning
Fierce flames reduce matter to white ash. The ego's shell cracks open in the fire. What must be burned away? What attachments need releasing?
"All impurities are purified by fire." —Sendivogius
Operation
Dissolution
Dissolving
Ash dissolves into dark waters. Rigid structures melt away. Where must you surrender control? What emotions need to flow?
"Water joins together everything dissolved." —Sendivogius
Operation
Separation
Discerning
A sword divides light from dark. Eagle rises, toad sinks. What must be separated? What distinction needs making?
"Fire separates everything that is joined." —Sendivogius
Operation
Conjunction
Uniting
King and Queen embrace. Sun and moon merge into one. Opposites unite in the vessel. What seeks integration within you?
"The animated spirit is joined to the body." —Khunrath
Operation
Fermentation
Inspiring
The peacock's tail shimmers with iridescent colors. Decay births new life. What inspiration is arising from crisis? What new growth stirs?
"Nature gives motion, motion excites fire." —Sendivogius
Operation
Distillation
Refining
Vapors rise through the alembic, condense, fall, rise again. Repeated cycles of refinement. What essence needs extracting?
"The tincture represents the Soul." —Drebbel
Operation
Coagulation
Manifesting
The distilled essence crystallizes into solid form. Spirit becomes matter. The Stone manifests. What is ready to take form?
"Let it slowly coagulate in a warm place." —Drebbel
Element
Fire
Will
Roaring flames, the salamander dancing within. The upward-pointing triangle. Where is the fire needed? What calls for transforming action?
"Fire acts in wonderful hidden ways." —Sendivogius
Element
Water
Emotion
Flowing streams, the undine spirit moving in depths. The downward-pointing triangle. What feelings need acknowledgment? What seeks to flow?
"Water purifies all imperfect things." —Sendivogius
Element
Air
Thought
Swirling winds, sylphs in flight. The triangle with crossbar. What thoughts need expression? What communication is required?
"Motion excites air, air excites fire." —Sendivogius
Element
Earth
Body
Mountains and caves, crystals growing in darkness. The inverted triangle with crossbar. What is the material reality? What needs grounding?
"Earth receives the Tincture." —Sendivogius
Element
Quintessence
Spirit
Starry cosmos, spiraling galaxy, celestial light beyond the four. The eternal, unchanging essence. What transcends? What is the spiritual core?
"The Quintessence is eternal, immutable." —Drebbel
Principle
Sulphur
Soul
Burning sun, golden flames, the solar king. The combustible principle, what gives life and desire. What does the soul want? What animates this?
"Sulphur, Salt, Mercury—the three Prima Materia." —Paracelsus
Principle
Mercury
Spirit
Winged messenger, caduceus staff, quicksilver flowing. What moves between realms? What message is being carried? Where is the mediating force?
"From living quicksilver comes the prima materia." —Rosicrucian MS
Principle
Salt
Body
White crystals, the cubic stone, what remains after fire. The fixed principle, the body that contains spirit. What is the form? What structure holds this?
"The Sun is fire, salt, and base." —Paracelsus
Vessel
Athanor
The Furnace
Tower oven with steady, patient flame. The egg-shaped vessel maintains constant heat over time. What requires sustained attention? Where is patience the path?
"With one Athanor furnace; and one fire." —Khunrath
Vessel
Alembic
The Still
Glass apparatus with curved head where vapors rise and condense. Drops of pure essence collect. What needs distilling? How to extract what matters?
"Extract tincture from the cucurbit." —Drebbel
Vessel
Crucible
The Test
Ceramic pot glowing red in fierce flame. Matter is tested, proved, transformed. What is being tested? What must withstand the fire?
"The body dies, turns black, reduced to ashes." —Khunrath
Vessel
Retort
The Return
Bent-neck flask returns condensed vapors to the body. The pelican feeding its young. What must circulate? What returns to source?
"It begins where it ends." —Hermes
Sage
Hermes
Trismegistus
Thrice-Great Hermes. The Emerald Tablet, the ibis, pyramids at dawn. As above, so below. How does the macrocosm mirror the microcosm?
"Man bore the image of his father." —Pymander
Sage
Paracelsus
The Physician
Swiss physician-alchemist with sword Azoth at his side. Nature is the teacher. What does nature teach here? What is the medicine needed?
"These three are Prima Materia, like God." —Paracelsus
Sage
Boehme
The Mystic
German shoemaker-mystic. Divine vision broke through while mending shoes. Aurora, the dawn light. What is the divine spark here?
"Heaven is the Sea of Fire, from the 7 Spirits." —Aurora
Sage
Ficino
The Translator
Florentine philosopher who brought Hermes to the Renaissance. The Platonic Academy, lyre music, candlelight over ancient texts. Where is beauty leading?
"Hermes attempted to eradicate this stain from souls." —On Pleasure
Sage
Sendivogius
The Adept
Polish adept-diplomat with practical laboratory wisdom. Understood nature's secret fire. What does practical wisdom say? What is the clear path?
"Fire acts in wonderful hidden ways." —New Chemical Light
Sage
Agrippa
The Magus
German polymath. Three Books of Occult Philosophy. Pentagram, celestial correspondences. What hidden forces are at work? What correspondences apply?
"God impresses the seal of Ideas upon Intelligences." —Occult Philosophy
Arcana
Prima Materia
First Matter
Chaos before creation, the cosmic egg floating in dark waters. Formless potential, raw material of the Great Work. What potential lies unformed?
"The Prima Materia is a divine mist." —Hellwig
Arcana
Lapis
The Stone
Radiant red stone, ruby crystal glowing with inner fire. Transmutes lead to gold, heals all disease. The perfected self. What would complete the work?
"He is praised in the Emerald Tablet... the living fire." —Hellwig
Arcana
Ouroboros
Eternal Return
Dragon in eternal circle, scales shifting from light to dark. End becomes beginning. Self-renewal through self-consumption. What cycle is completing?
"It begins where it ends." —Hermes
Arcana
Rebis
The Double
Hermaphrodite figure, two heads on one body, sun and moon crowning left and right. Perfect union of opposites. What dualities seek resolution?
"Man bore the image of his father." —Pymander
Arcana
Elixir
The Medicine
Golden liquid in a crystal vial, glowing with life. Panacea, the drinkable gold. Universal medicine for body and soul. What healing is needed?
"The tincture heals all diseases... represents the Soul." —Drebbel
THE GREAT WORK
A 4-card spread mapping transformation
1. Nigredo — What must die or be faced
2. Albedo — What is being purified
3. Citrinitas — What wisdom emerges
4. Rubedo — What completion is possible
Draw four cards. Read each through its position's alchemical stage.
THE CRUCIBLE
A 3-card spread for times of transformation
1. Dissolving — What is breaking down
2. Emerging — What is taking form
3. Catalyst — What accelerates change
Focus on a situation in flux. Draw three cards for transformation dynamics.
AS ABOVE, SO BELOW
A 2-card spread for correspondences
1. Above — The spiritual/mental dimension
2. Below — The material/physical dimension
Draw two cards. Consider how they mirror and inform each other.
SINGLE DRAW
Daily contemplation practice:

Morning: Shuffle and draw one card. Sit with its image and question.

Day: Notice where its themes appear in your experience.

Evening: Journal what you observed. What transformation is present?
