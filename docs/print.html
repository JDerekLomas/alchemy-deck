Alchemy Deck - Printable Oracle Cards

34 oracle cards + 5 prompt cards | Print double-sided, flip on long edge

Quotes from primary sources at sourcelibrary.org

Stage
Nigredo
Blackening
The first stage of transformation. Something must die for the new to be born. What needs to end? What shadow are you avoiding? The dark night holds the seed of dawn.
"I am black, white, yellow, and red." —Maier
Stage
Albedo
Whitening
Purification after darkness. The impurities wash away, revealing what was hidden beneath. What clarity is emerging? What false beliefs are dissolving?
"Water purifies all imperfect things." —Sendivogius
Stage
Citrinitas
Yellowing
Solar consciousness awakens. After purification comes illumination—the first glimpse of wisdom. What insight is breaking through? What understanding is dawning?
"The vulture cries: I am white and black, yellow and red." —Maier
Stage
Rubedo
Reddening
The Great Work complete. Opposites unite, the self becomes whole. Integration of all that was separated. What would wholeness look like here?
"The fixed becomes volatile; the volatile, fixed." —Khunrath
Operation
Calcination
Burning
Reduction by fire. Ego attachments, false identities, outdated beliefs—all fuel for the flames. What must be burned away? What are you clinging to?
"All impurities are purified by fire." —Sendivogius
Operation
Dissolution
Dissolving
Letting go into the waters. Rigid structures soften, defenses melt, emotions finally flow. Where are you holding too tightly? What needs releasing?
"Water joins together everything dissolved." —Sendivogius
Operation
Separation
Discerning
Sorting the essential from the inessential. Not all that dissolved belongs together. What serves your growth? What must be left behind?
"Fire separates everything that is joined." —Sendivogius
Operation
Conjunction
Uniting
The sacred marriage within. What was separated now reunites at a higher level. Masculine and feminine, light and shadow. What opposites in you seek union?
"The animated spirit is joined to the body." —Khunrath
Operation
Fermentation
Inspiring
New life from decay. The breakdown creates conditions for breakthrough. Crisis becomes catalyst. What is fermenting in you? What inspiration arises from difficulty?
"Nature gives motion, motion excites fire." —Sendivogius
Operation
Distillation
Refining
Repeated cycles of purification. Rise, condense, return, rise again. Each pass extracts more essence. What needs further refinement? What truth is becoming clearer?
"The tincture represents the Soul." —Drebbel
Operation
Coagulation
Manifesting
Spirit takes form. The refined essence crystallizes into reality. Insight becomes action, vision becomes creation. What is ready to manifest?
"Let it slowly coagulate in a warm place." —Drebbel
Element
Fire
Will
The principle of transformation and will. Fire purifies, energizes, and destroys what cannot withstand truth. Where do you need more fire? What requires decisive action?
"Fire acts in wonderful hidden ways." —Sendivogius
Element
Water
Emotion
The principle of feeling and intuition. Water dissolves, cleanses, and connects. It flows around obstacles and finds the lowest places. What emotions need honoring?
"Water purifies all imperfect things." —Sendivogius
Element
Air
Thought
The principle of mind and communication. Air carries words, ideas, and breath itself. It moves between all things. What needs to be said? What thoughts seek expression?
"Motion excites air, air excites fire." —Sendivogius
Element
Earth
Body
The principle of matter and manifestation. Earth grounds, stabilizes, and gives form. It is the body, the material world. What is the physical reality here?
"Earth receives the Tincture." —Sendivogius
Element
Quintessence
Spirit
The fifth element, beyond the four. The eternal essence that animates all matter, unchanged by transformation. What is the spiritual dimension here?
"The Quintessence is eternal, immutable." —Drebbel
Principle
Sulphur
Soul
The combustible principle—what burns, desires, and animates. The soul's fire, the driving force within. What does your soul truly want here?
"Sulphur, Salt, Mercury—the three Prima Materia." —Paracelsus
Principle
Mercury
Spirit
The volatile principle—what moves, mediates, and transforms. The messenger between realms, ever-changing quicksilver. What is in flux? What message is trying to get through?
"From living quicksilver comes the prima materia." —Rosicrucian MS
Principle
Salt
Body
The fixed principle—what remains, preserves, and gives form. The body that contains soul and spirit. What is the structure here? What form is this taking?
"The Sun is fire, salt, and base." —Paracelsus
Vessel
Athanor
The Furnace
The furnace of slow, steady heat. Transformation requires sustained warmth over time, not quick flames. What requires your patient attention? Where is patience the path?
"With one Athanor furnace; and one fire." —Khunrath
Vessel
Alembic
The Still
The vessel of distillation. Vapors rise, condense, and the essence is collected drop by drop. What needs to be distilled from this situation? What is essential?
"Extract tincture from the cucurbit." —Drebbel
Vessel
Crucible
The Test
The vessel of trial by fire. Here matter is tested and proved. Only what is genuine survives. What is being tested? What will remain when the fire has done its work?
"The body dies, turns black, reduced to ashes." —Khunrath
Vessel
Retort
The Return
The vessel of circulation. What rises must return to the body, again and again, each cycle deepening the work. What keeps returning? What pattern needs completing?
"It begins where it ends." —Hermes
Sage
Hermes
Trismegistus
As above, so below. The legendary founder taught that the macrocosm mirrors the microcosm. What pattern in your life reflects a larger truth?
"Man bore the image of his father." —Pymander
Sage
Paracelsus
The Physician
Nature is the teacher; experience over authority. The radical physician sought the medicine hidden in nature. What is the medicine here? What does direct experience teach?
"These three are Prima Materia, like God." —Paracelsus
Sage
Boehme
The Mystic
The divine spark in ordinary life. A shoemaker's mystical vision revealed spirit hidden in matter. Where is the sacred in your everyday? What vision might break through?
"Heaven is the Sea of Fire, from the 7 Spirits." —Aurora
Sage
Ficino
The Translator
Beauty leads the soul upward. The philosopher who revived Hermes taught that love of beauty opens the path to wisdom. What beauty is calling you?
"Hermes attempted to eradicate this stain from souls." —On Pleasure
Sage
Sendivogius
The Adept
Work with nature, not against it. The practical adept taught that nature's secret fire works in hidden ways. What is the natural path forward?
"Fire acts in wonderful hidden ways." —New Chemical Light
Sage
Agrippa
The Magus
Hidden correspondences connect all things. The magus mapped occult virtues linking stars, stones, plants, and souls. What hidden connections are at work here?
"God impresses the seal of Ideas upon Intelligences." —Occult Philosophy
Arcana
Prima Materia
First Matter
The raw material before the work begins. Chaos, potential, the formless substance from which all emerges. What is the starting point here? What raw potential awaits?
"The Prima Materia is a divine mist." —Hellwig
Arcana
Lapis
The Stone
The goal of the Great Work. The perfected self that transforms everything it touches. Not an end state but a living process. What does completion mean here?
"He is praised in the Emerald Tablet... the living fire." —Hellwig
Arcana
Ouroboros
Eternal Return
The serpent eating its tail. Endings become beginnings, destruction feeds creation. The cycle completes itself. What cycle is ending and beginning?
"It begins where it ends." —Hermes
Arcana
Rebis
The Double
The union of opposites in one being. Not compromise but integration at a higher level—both/and rather than either/or. What dualities in you seek reconciliation?
"Man bore the image of his father." —Pymander
Arcana
Elixir
The Medicine
The universal medicine that heals all ills. Not a substance but a state—the perfected tincture that restores wholeness. What healing is needed here?
"The tincture heals all diseases... represents the Soul." —Drebbel
THE GREAT WORK
A 4-card spread mapping transformation
1. Nigredo — What must die or be faced
2. Albedo — What is being purified
3. Citrinitas — What wisdom emerges
4. Rubedo — What completion is possible
Draw four cards. Read each through its position's alchemical stage.
THE CRUCIBLE
A 3-card spread for times of transformation
1. Dissolving — What is breaking down
2. Emerging — What is taking form
3. Catalyst — What accelerates change
Focus on a situation in flux. Draw three cards for transformation dynamics.
AS ABOVE, SO BELOW
A 2-card spread for correspondences
1. Above — The spiritual/mental dimension
2. Below — The material/physical dimension
Draw two cards. Consider how they mirror and inform each other.
SINGLE DRAW
Daily contemplation practice:

Morning: Shuffle and draw one card. Sit with its image and question.

Day: Notice where its themes appear in your experience.

Evening: Journal what you observed. What transformation is present?
